Vibrant Shopping Scene

Immense yourself in the charming city and explore its diverse and exciting shopping options. From trendy boutiques to local artisanal markets, our guide showcases the best shopping destinations in Charleston.

Juliska

Discover Juliska’s artisanal tableware, barware, home décor, jewelry & gifts in our flagship store — home to a table-setting studio, registry parlor & creative workshop space.

Historic Charleston Museum Shop

Locally-made gifts and packages including jewelry, furnishings, Charleston-related books and tasty treats. Enjoy tax-free shopping and support local preservation efforts.

Freshfields Village

Freshfields Village is a true town-center offering a unique mix of shopping, dining, service, and lodging establishments all shaped by Lowcountry tradition and high style.

The Shops @ Charleston Place

An exclusive collection of world-famous stores in the heart of downtown Charleston. The ultimate shopping experience & the finest selections in the city.

Rhodes Boutique

A specialty boutique offering ladies apparel, home décor, baby gifts, bridal, and more. We offer numerous embroidery services from personal to corporate orders.

Jordan Lash Charleston

Charleston's premier men’s clothing store with outstanding brands, unparalleled service & great style. Shop with confidence and ease on Historic King Street.

Celadon

Head to our Warehouse & Outlet location to shop new arrivals before they head to our Main store or find ding, dent and discontinued pieces at great discounted prices!

Firefly Distillery

Firefly offers over 30 innovative spirits, including distillery exclusives, and an expansive Gift Shop featuring mixers, apparel, bar tools, and local Charleston products.

Firefly Distillery

 4201 Spruill Avenue North Charleston, SC 29405
 (843) 557-1405
 fireflydistillery.com | info@fireflyvodka.com

Temporary Hours:
12:00PM – 6:00PM Monday-Saturday

Surrounded by oaks and marsh views, the newly-built Firefly Distillery captures the true essence of the Lowcountry in the heart of Charleston area. The birthplace of Firefly Spirits and the world's first sweet tea vodka is South Carolina’s largest distillery. Experience the distillation process first hand with tours and sample Firefly Spirits in the tasting room, including distillery specialties such as the new small batch bourbon. Enjoy good times and great spirits, kick back marsh-side with live music and local food trucks and stay a while.
