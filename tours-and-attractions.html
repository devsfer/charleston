Looking for things to do in Charleston?

Charleston is a city with a rich history and vibrant culture, and there's no better way to experience it than through our diverse selection of tours and attractions. From historic walking tours to culinary excursions, there's something for everyone in Charleston.

One of our most popular tours is the historic district walking tour, where you can learn about the city's colonial past and see some of its most iconic landmarks, such as the Battery and Rainbow Row. For those interested in Charleston's haunted history, our ghost tours are a must-see. You'll hear spooky tales of the city's past and visit some of its most haunted locations.

If you're a foodie, we have plenty of culinary tours that will take you to some of Charleston's best restaurants and markets. You can sample traditional Lowcountry cuisine, like shrimp and grits and she-crab soup, and learn about the city's culinary traditions and history.

In addition to tours, Charleston has plenty of attractions that are worth visiting. The Charleston City Market is a historic landmark that dates back to the 1800s and is home to hundreds of vendors selling everything from handcrafted goods to local delicacies. The South Carolina Aquarium is another popular attraction that showcases the state's diverse marine life and features interactive exhibits for all ages.

No matter what your interests are, Charleston has something to offer. Be sure to check out our tours and attractions section to plan your perfect Charleston vacation.
