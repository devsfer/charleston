 THE HOLY CITY 

As you wander the gas lamp-lit streets of Charleston at night, passing horse-drawn carriages, you may feel as if you have been transported back in time. This South Carolina city is proud to celebrate its centuries-old mansions, Spanish moss-draped trees, spooky cemeteries, and cobblestone alleys. However, Charleston is far from stuck in the past; it is home to innovative restaurants, interesting shops, contemporary art galleries, and the world-class Spoleto Festival. In a word: Charleston.
